# SalePaste

**Copy a price. Paste your price.**

SalePaste is a lightweight macOS menu bar app that automatically adjusts prices as you copy and paste them — no calculator, no spreadsheet, no manual math. Built for resellers, dropshippers, freelancers, and anyone who reprices items throughout the day.

---

## Why SalePaste?

If you spend your day copying prices from one place and pasting a marked-up (or discounted) version somewhere else — a supplier listing into your storefront, a wholesale price into a quote, a cost into a sale price — you're doing the same math over and over. SalePaste does it for you, instantly, the moment you copy.

- Copy `129.99` from a supplier page
- SalePaste applies your markup automatically
- Paste `139.09` wherever you need it

No app switching. No typing numbers into a calculator. It just works in the background.

## Features

- **Watches your clipboard** — SalePaste quietly detects when you copy a price, with zero setup.
- **Automatic markup or discount** — set a custom percentage once, and every price you copy gets adjusted before you paste.
- **Flexible rounding** — round up, down, or leave it exact, to the nearest 1, 5, or 10.
- **Lives in your menu bar** — a simple, unobtrusive icon that turns green when active and red when paused, so you always know its status at a glance.
- **One click to pause** — toggle SalePaste on or off instantly from the menu bar or the main window.
- **Fast, native, and lightweight** — built entirely with SwiftUI for macOS, with no background bloat.

## Privacy first

SalePaste works entirely on your Mac. There is no networking code in the app — your clipboard contents are never uploaded, shared, tracked, or synced anywhere. Everything happens locally, instantly, and stays that way.

- macOS 26 or later

## How it works

1. Set your markup or discount percentage (and optional rounding) in the SalePaste window.
2. Copy any price-like number to your clipboard.
3. SalePaste recalculates it in place — paste it anywhere, already adjusted.
4. Toggle SalePaste off anytime from the menu bar if you need to copy something without adjustment.
